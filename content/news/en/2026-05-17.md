## AI Industry Overview

A busy week across the AI landscape. OpenAI launched GPT-5.5 Instant and unified ChatGPT, Codex, and its developer API under co-founder Greg Brockman. Google previewed "Gemini Intelligence" for Android 17 — pushing past the chatbot paradigm toward a full OS-level AI layer — just weeks before Apple's anticipated AI reboot at WWDC. Anthropic expanded its PwC partnership to deploy Claude across hundreds of thousands of professionals. Chinese AI firms saw major funding moves: DeepSeek is reportedly raising $4B from China's state AI fund, Kimi-maker Moonshot raised $2B at a $20B valuation, and Kling AI is eyeing a separate IPO at a ~$20B valuation. Meanwhile, the UK government launched its Claude-powered GOV.UK Chat to 560,000+ users, and AI cybersecurity reached a milestone as Google identified the first AI-developed zero-day exploit.

## OpenAI / ChatGPT

OpenAI released GPT-5.5 Instant, described as smarter, more personalized, and more autonomous ([OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-5-instant/)). The company also launched a personal finance feature allowing ChatGPT to connect to users' bank accounts for financial advice ([Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/technology/tech-news/chatgpt-can-now-look-at-your-bank-account-heres-what-it-can-do/articleshow/131131865.cms)). In a major organizational shift, OpenAI unified ChatGPT, Codex, and its developer API under co-founder Greg Brockman, positioning the company ahead of Google I/O ([TechTimes](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/316730/20260516/openai-unifies-chatgpt-codex-developer-api-under-co-founder-brockman-four-days-before-google-i-o.htm)). OpenAI also shut down Sora and the DALL·E 3 API, redirecting compute to coding and enterprise tools ([Silicon Report](https://www.siliconreport.com/openai-shuts-down-sora-and-redirects-compute-to-coding-and-enterprise-tools-aa0540441d9f2299)).

## Google Gemini

Google unveiled "Gemini Intelligence" — a suite of agentic AI features for Android 17 that can move across apps, understand screen context, and complete multi-step tasks like building shopping carts or booking reservations, with the user always in the loop ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/12/google-races-put-gemini-at-center-of-android-before-apples-ai-reboot.html)). The rollout includes Gemini in Chrome for Android, AI-powered Gboard dictation ("Rambler"), and the ability to vibe-code Android widgets by describing them in natural language ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/12/google-brings-agentic-ai-and-vibe-coded-widgets-to-android/)). Separately, 9to5Google reported that Google is developing "Gemini Spark," a new AI agent feature ([9to5Google](https://9to5google.com/2026/05/14/gemini-spark-insight/)).

## Anthropic Claude

Anthropic and PwC expanded their strategic alliance to deploy Claude Code and Claude Cowork across a global workforce of hundreds of thousands of professionals, focusing on agentic technology builds, AI-native deal-making, and enterprise function reinvention — cutting delivery times by up to 70% ([Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/pwc-expanded-partnership)). Anthropic also expanded Claude's specialized tools for law firms and lawyers, including contract review and legal research capabilities ([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/legal/litigation/anthropic-expands-claudes-ai-tools-law-firms-lawyers-2026-05-12/)). In the UK, the government's GOV.UK Chat — powered by Claude and available to 563,000+ app users — went live, providing AI-assisted answers drawn from 80,000+ pages of official government guidance ([PublicTechnology](https://www.publictechnology.net/2026/05/15/society-and-welfare/government-ai-chatbot-goes-live-across-gov-uk-app/)).

## AI Cybersecurity Milestone

Google's Threat Intelligence Group identified the first known AI-developed zero-day exploit and thwarted a planned mass exploitation event before it launched. The exploit — a Python script bypassing 2FA — contained hallucinated CVSS scores and textbook-formatted docstrings characteristic of LLM output. Google's defensive AI agent "Big Sleep" found the same vulnerability first, preventing the attack ([TNW](https://thenextweb.com/news/google-ai-zero-day-exploit-cybersecurity-arms-race)).
