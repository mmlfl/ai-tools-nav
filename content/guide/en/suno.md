## Overview

Suno is a leading AI music generation platform that creates complete songs - vocals, instrumentation, and production - from text prompts. It can generate music across virtually any genre.

## Core Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Text-to-Music | Generate complete songs from text descriptions |
| Lyric Generation | AI writes original lyrics on any topic or style |
| Custom Lyrics | Write your own lyrics, AI composes the music |
| Genre Flexibility | Generate music in virtually any genre |
| Vocal Control | Specify vocal style, gender, and delivery |
| Song Extension | Extend generated songs for longer versions |

## How to Use

1. Sign up at suno.ai
2. Click "Create" and describe your song
3. Example prompt: "An upbeat pop song about summer road trips with catchy chorus"
4. Use Custom Mode to write your own lyrics
5. Listen to both variations, extend the best one

## Pricing

| Plan | Price/mo | Credits |
|------|----------|---------|
| Basic | Free | 50 credits/day (5 gens) |
| Pro | $10 | 2,500 credits |

## Pros and Cons

**Pros**: Remarkably complete music generation, extremely easy to use, wide genre coverage, custom lyrics support, fun creative tool.
**Cons**: Variable audio quality, limited control over specific elements, copyright considerations, web-only (no API).
