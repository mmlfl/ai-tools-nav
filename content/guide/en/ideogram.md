## How to Use Ideogram: Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. **Account Setup & Interface Navigation**
   - **Sign Up**: Visit [ideogram.ai](https://ideogram.ai) and create a free account (no credit card required).
   - **Dashboard**: The interface features three tabs: *Create* (main workspace), *Gallery* (history), and *Credits* (usage tracker).
   - **Key Controls**: 
     - *Prompt Box*: Where you describe your image. **Pro Tip:** Always enclose exact text in quotes (e.g., "‘SALE 50% OFF’ in bold red font").
     - *Style Selector*: Choose from preset styles or type "custom style: [description]".
     - *Aspect Ratio*: Select 1:1 (social), 16:9 (banners), or custom dimensions.
     - *Advanced Settings*: Toggle "Text Focus" (prioritizes text clarity) and "Seed" (reproduce results).

### 2. **Crafting Effective Text Prompts**
   - **Basic Structure**: `[Subject] with [text content] in [font style] on [background]`
     - *Example:* "Modern coffee cup with ‘BEST BREW’ in sans-serif font on wooden background"
   - **Critical Tips for Text Clarity**:
     - Use **single quotes** around exact phrases (e.g., "‘20% OFF’ in bold italic").
     - Specify font traits: *"handwritten script," "futuristic digital font," "serif for luxury feel"*
     - Add positioning cues: *"text centered at top," "logo in bottom-right corner"*
     - Avoid complex layouts (e.g., curved text)—simplify to straight lines for best results.
   - **Advanced Technique**: For multilingual content, prefix text with language code:  
     `‘こんにちは’ (Japanese) in clean typeface on gradient background`

### 3. **Generating & Refining Images**
   1. Enter your prompt with text specifications.
   2. Select style (e.g., "Minimalist" for logos) and aspect ratio.
   3. Enable **Text Focus** for text-heavy images.
   4. Click *Generate* and wait 3-5 seconds.
   5. **If text is imperfect**:
      - *Revise prompt*: Add "crisp," "high contrast," or "no distortion" (e.g., "‘WELCOME’ in crisp white letters on dark blue").
      - *Use negative prompts*: Exclude "blurry text," "distorted letters" in advanced settings.
      - *Regenerate with seed*: Lock successful settings using the seed value for consistency.

### 4. **Exporting & Post-Processing**
   - **Download Options**: 
     - *Standard*: PNG/JPEG (free)
     - *Pro Feature*: Transparent background (for logos) and vector-ready SVG (requires Pro plan)
   - **Batch Processing**: Generate 4 variants at once by toggling "Multiple Outputs" (Pro feature).
   - **Edit in Gallery**: 
     - Use *Upscale* to enhance resolution without losing text clarity.
     - *Remix* to tweak prompts while preserving composition.

**Pro Workflow for Logos**:  
1. Prompt: "Abstract ‘A’ logo with ‘AlphaTech’ in Montserrat font, minimalist style, white background"  
2. Regenerate 3x using "Remix" to adjust spacing.  
3. Export as SVG (Pro plan) for vector editing in Illustrator.  
4. Use *Color Swapper* (Pro feature) to test brand color variations.
