## Overview

ElevenLabs is the leading AI text-to-speech and voice cloning platform, offering studio-quality speech synthesis in 29+ languages. Used for podcasts, audiobooks, video voiceovers, localization, and accessibility.

## Core Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Text-to-Speech | Natural-sounding speech in 29+ languages |
| Voice Cloning | Create AI replicas of real voices (instant or professional) |
| Voice Library | Browse thousands of community-shared voices |
| Speech-to-Speech | Transform your voice while preserving emotion |
| Voice Design | Create entirely new synthetic voices |
| Dubbing Studio | Automated video dubbing with voice matching |

## How to Use

1. Sign up at elevenlabs.io
2. Select a voice or clone your own in VoiceLab
3. Enter text and adjust voice settings
4. Stability: 0.5-0.75 (balanced), Clarity: 0.7-0.85
5. Generate and download the audio file
