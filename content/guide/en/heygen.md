## How to Use
Follow these steps to create your first professional video:

### Step 1: Account Setup & Avatar Selection
1. **Sign up** at [heygen.com](https://www.heygen.com) using your email or Google account.  
2. **Choose an avatar**:  
   - *Beginners*: Use the "Discover" tab to browse pre-built avatars (e.g., "Emma," a corporate presenter).  
   - *Advanced*: Upload a custom photo under "Create Avatar" (requires 10+ clear face images).  
3. **Customize**: Adjust hair color, clothing, and background via the editor. Pro tip: Use "Professional" background templates for sales videos to boost credibility.

### Step 2: Script & Translation Workflow
1. **Add content**:  
   - *For new videos*: Click "Create Video" > "Text-to-Video" and paste your script.  
   - *For existing videos*: Use "Video Translate" to upload a .mp4 file (max 5 mins).  
2. **Localize**:  
   - Select target language (e.g., Spanish).  
   - Toggle "Preserve original speaker’s emotion" to maintain tone.  
   - Review auto-generated translation; edit nuances like "hello" → "¡Hola!" for cultural relevance.  
3. **Generate**: Click "Create Video" and wait 3-8 minutes (processing time varies by length).

### Step 3: Advanced Editing & Export
1. **Refine timing**:  
   - Drag timeline handles to extend pauses between sentences.  
   - Use "Emotion Boost" sliders to intensify smiles or nods (critical for persuasive content).  
2. **Add polish**:  
   - Insert B-roll via the "Media Library" (e.g., product shots for marketing videos).  
   - Apply "Cinematic" filter for education videos to increase viewer retention by 22% (per HeyGen’s 2024 case study).  
3. **Export**:  
   - Select 1080p for YouTube or 9:16 for TikTok/Instagram Reels.  
   - Enable "Download SRT" to get subtitles for accessibility compliance.  

**Pro Tip**: For multilingual campaigns, use the "Batch Translate" feature. Upload one English video, select 5 target languages, and generate all versions simultaneously. This cuts localization time by 70% compared to manual workflows.
