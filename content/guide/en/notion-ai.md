## Overview

Notion AI is an AI assistant integrated into the Notion workspace. It enhances Notion's note-taking, documentation, and database capabilities with AI writing, editing, summarization, and Q&A features.

## Core Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| AI Writing | Generate, continue, or rewrite content in any Notion page |
| AI Editing | Improve grammar, change tone, translate, simplify, expand |
| Summarization | Summarize long pages, meeting notes, or documents |
| Q&A | Ask questions about your workspace and get answers |
| Translation | Translate content between multiple languages |
| Database AI | AI-powered properties, formula generation, autofill |
| Brainstorming | Generate ideas, outlines, and pros/cons lists |

## How to Use

1. Enable Notion AI as an add-on to any Notion plan
2. In pages: press Space on empty line or type /ai
3. Select text for AI actions (improve, translate, summarize, etc.)
4. Use Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+K for Q&A about your workspace
5. Add AI properties and autofill to databases
