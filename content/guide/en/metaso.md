## Overview

MetaSo AI (秘塔 AI) is a next-generation AI search engine designed to transcend traditional keyword-based search results. Unlike conventional search engines that overwhelm users with endless links, MetaSo delivers structured, synthesized answers with visual mind maps—making it ideal for researchers, students, and professionals conducting in-depth investigations. Developed by the Chinese company Mituo (秘塔科技), this ad-free platform processes queries across Chinese and English sources, reorganizing fragmented information into coherent knowledge frameworks. Its unique value lies in eliminating advertising noise while providing instant context and connections between concepts, transforming how users absorb complex information.

While primarily optimized for Chinese-language content, MetaSo’s English capabilities have rapidly improved, making it a versatile tool for global users. The platform excels at academic research, market analysis, and learning complex topics by distilling information from scholarly papers, news archives, and authoritative websites into digestible formats. Unlike ChatGPT or other LLMs that generate answers from training data, MetaSo actively searches the web in real-time, ensuring up-to-date results with source citations. This guide will walk you through leveraging its full potential—from basic queries to advanced research workflows—helping you unlock deeper insights with minimal effort.

## How to Use

Follow these steps to maximize MetaSo’s capabilities. All actions start at [https://metaso.cn](https://metaso.cn).

### Step 1: Access & Interface Navigation
- **For English users**: Click the globe icon → select "English" (top-right corner). Note: Some advanced features may default to Chinese.  
- **Key UI elements**:  
  - *Search bar*: Type queries (e.g., "Explain quantum computing for beginners").  
  - *Answer panel*: Main results with source citations.  
  - *Mind map toggle*: Button labeled "思维导图" (top-right of results) to activate visualization.  
  - *Source filter*: Adjust credibility thresholds (e.g., "Academic Only") via the settings menu.  

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: Use `/` before queries to trigger specific modes. Example: `/search renewable energy trends 2024` forces web search over AI synthesis.

### Step 2: Formulating Effective Queries
MetaSo works best with specific, context-rich questions:  
- ❌ Weak: "AI"  
- ✅ Strong: "Compare transformer vs. RNN architectures for NLP in 2024, with sources from IEEE or ACM"  

**Advanced query syntax**:  
- `site:.edu` → Limits to academic domains  
- `after:2023` → Filters for recent results  
- `"exact phrase"` → Forces exact matches  

> **Example Workflow**:  
> *Query*: "How does CRISPR-Cas9 gene editing work? Show a mind map with ethical considerations from WHO and Nature."  
> *Result*: Structured answer + mind map connecting "mechanism," "applications," and "ethics" with WHO/Nature citations.

### Step 3: Interacting with Results
- **Expand answers**: Click "..." under any section to reveal detailed explanations.  
- **Source verification**: Hover over citation links to see source reliability scores (e.g., "95% Credible" for peer-reviewed journals).  
- **Compare viewpoints**: For controversial topics (e.g., "GMO safety"), click "Compare Sources" to see 3 perspectives side-by-side.  
- **Refine queries**: Use the "Improve Query" button to auto-generate more precise versions of your question.

### Step 4: Leveraging Mind Maps
1. Run any query (e.g., "Causes of climate change").  
2. Click the mind map icon.  
3. **Interact**:  
   - *Zoom*: Scroll to magnify clusters.  
   - *Expand*: Click central nodes (e.g., "Human Causes") to drill into subtopics.  
   - *Export*: Right-click → "Save as PNG" for presentations.  
4. **Customize**: Click "Edit" to add your own nodes or delete irrelevant branches.  

> ⚠️ **Note**: Mind maps require registration (free). Use your email or WeChat to save customizations.

### Step 5: Advanced Research Workflows
1. **Upload documents**: Click "Upload" → add a PDF → ask "Summarize key findings in 3 bullet points."  
2. **Multi-query chaining**:  
   - First query: "List top 5 AI trends in healthcare 2024"  
   - Second query: "Explain [Trend #3] with mind map" (using the answer from Step 1)  
3. **Export for collaboration**:  
   - Click "Share" → generate a link with live mind map.  
   - Or export as Markdown to import into Notion/obsidian.  

**Real-World Example**:  
*Task*: Research "blockchain applications in supply chain management" for a university paper.  
*Steps*:  
1. Query: "Blockchain in supply chain: case studies from IBM and Maersk after 2022"  
2. Click "Compare Sources" to see IBM’s vs. Maersk’s approaches.  
3. Generate mind map → export as PNG for paper appendix.  
4. Use "Cite" button to auto-format references in APA style.  

This process reduces 3 hours of manual research to 15 minutes while ensuring credible sourcing.
