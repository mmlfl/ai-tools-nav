## Overview

Sora is OpenAI's groundbreaking AI video generation model that creates realistic videos from text descriptions. It generates complex scenes with accurate physics, motion, and character consistency up to 60 seconds.

## Core Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Text-to-Video | Generate videos from natural language descriptions |
| Image-to-Video | Animate still images into video clips |
| Video Extension | Extend videos forward or backward in time |
| Scene Composition | Complex multi-character, multi-object scenes |
| Physics Simulation | Realistic object motion and interaction |
| Style Transfer | Apply artistic styles to generated videos |

## How to Use

1. Available to ChatGPT Plus and Pro subscribers
2. Describe the scene in detail: characters, setting, action, camera movement, mood
3. Include camera directions: "Slow pan", "Close-up", "Aerial view"
4. Specify visual style: "Cinematic", "Documentary style", "Animation"
5. Iterate on prompts to refine results

## Pricing

| Access | Price |
|--------|-------|
| ChatGPT Plus | Included ($20/month) - limited generations |
| ChatGPT Pro | Included ($200/month) - more, higher quality |

## Pros and Cons

**Pros**: Unprecedented video quality and realism, strong physics understanding, up to 60s generation, ChatGPT integration.
**Cons**: Limited availability (subscription required), long generation times, content restrictions, no developer API.
