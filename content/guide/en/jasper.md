## Overview

Jasper is an AI marketing content platform for teams creating at scale. It offers brand voice management, SEO optimization, campaign orchestration, and team collaboration - purpose-built for marketing workflows.

## Core Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Brand Voice | Train AI on your brand's tone, style, and terminology |
| Document Editor | Full editor with AI writing, rewriting, and expansion |
| Jasper Chat | Conversational AI for brainstorming and ideation |
| SEO Mode | Integrated SEO optimization with keyword targeting |
| Templates | 50+ marketing templates (blogs, ads, emails, social) |
| Campaigns | Create multi-channel campaigns with consistent messaging |
| AI Images | Generate marketing images within the platform |

## How to Use

1. Start trial at jasper.ai
2. Set up Brand Voice: upload content examples, define tone and terminology
3. Create content using templates or the document editor
4. Use SEO Mode to optimize for target keywords
5. Manage campaigns with team collaboration and approvals

**Pros**: Purpose-built for marketing, powerful brand voice system, integrated SEO, comprehensive campaign management, good team features.
**Cons**: Expensive vs general AI tools, no permanent free tier, learning curve for full feature set.
