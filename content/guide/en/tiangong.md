## Overview

Tiangong AI (天工 AI), developed by Chinese technology company Kunlun Tech, is an all-in-one AI platform that seamlessly merges advanced search capabilities with generative AI tools. Launched in 2023, this innovative solution addresses the growing demand for integrated AI services by combining real-time web search with content creation, coding assistance, and multilingual support in a single interface. Unlike standalone chatbots or search engines, Tiangong AI processes user queries through a dual-layer system: first retrieving the most relevant and current information from the web, then synthesizing it into coherent, context-aware responses. This unique architecture makes it particularly valuable for users who need both factual accuracy and creative output, whether they're conducting academic research, drafting marketing copy, or debugging code.

The platform has rapidly gained traction in Chinese markets while expanding its global accessibility, with English language support becoming increasingly robust. Its strength lies in bridging the gap between traditional search engines and generative AI – a critical differentiator in today's information landscape where outdated knowledge bases plague many AI assistants. Tiangong AI's real-time data integration ensures users receive current information without needing to cross-reference multiple sources. While initially designed for Chinese-speaking users, its English capabilities have matured significantly, making it a compelling option for international users seeking an alternative to Western AI platforms, especially those requiring up-to-date information that older models might lack.

## How to Use

### Step 1: Accessing the Platform
1. Navigate to [https://www.tiangong.cn](https://www.tiangong.cn) in any modern browser
2. No account is required for basic search functionality, but creating one unlocks additional features
3. To register: Click "Sign Up" in the top-right corner, use your email or social media account (WeChat, QQ, or international options available)
4. Verify your email to activate full features (takes 2-5 minutes)

### Step 2: Performing AI-Powered Searches
1. Enter your query in the central search bar (e.g., "latest developments in renewable energy 2024")
2. Press Enter or click the search icon
3. Review the AI-generated summary at the top (this synthesizes key points from multiple sources)
4. Examine the cited search results below – each includes source credibility indicators
5. Click "View Source" on any result to see the original webpage
6. For deeper analysis, click "Explain This" on any result to get a simplified breakdown

*Pro Tip:* Add "in English" to your query for better English responses (e.g., "Explain quantum computing in English"). The system prioritizes language based on query phrasing.

### Step 3: Generating Custom Content
1. From the homepage, click "Create" in the top menu
2. Select your content type (article, email, social post, etc.)
3. In the prompt field, provide specific instructions:
   - *Good:* "Write a 500-word LinkedIn post about AI ethics for tech executives, professional tone, include 3 statistics"
   - *Bad:* "Write about AI ethics"
4. Adjust settings using sliders for:
   - Creativity (low for factual content, high for creative writing)
   - Length (short/medium/long)
   - Formality (casual to formal)
5. Click "Generate" and review the output
6. Use the "Regenerate" button for alternative versions or "Improve" for refinement

*Example Workflow:* To create a market analysis report:
1. Search "Q2 2024 smartphone market trends"
2. Copy the AI summary and top 3 sources
3. Go to "Create" > "Report"
4. Paste sources into the prompt field with instructions: "Create a 1,000-word analysis using these sources, focus on Apple and Samsung market share changes, include data visualization suggestions"

### Step 4: Using the Code Assistant
1. In the search bar, type "code:" followed by your request (e.g., "code: write a Python function to calculate Fibonacci sequence")
2. For complex projects, use the dedicated coding interface (accessible via "Tools" > "Code")
3. Input your programming language, problem description, and constraints
4. Review the generated code with line-by-line explanations
5. Click "Debug" to identify errors in your own code snippets
6. Use "Explain" to get detailed breakdowns of complex algorithms

*Advanced Technique:* For debugging:
1. Paste your error message
2. Add context: "I'm getting this error in a React app when clicking a button"
3. Include your component code (up to 1,000 characters)
4. Tiangong AI will identify the issue and suggest fixes with explanations

### Step 5: Leveraging Multilingual Capabilities
1. Type "Translate" followed by your text (e.g., "Translate this to English: 今天天气很好")
2. For professional translation: 
   - Click "Create" > "Translation"
   - Upload documents (PDF, Word)
   - Select industry-specific terminology (legal, medical, technical)
3. To maintain cultural context: Add "for [audience]" to prompts (e.g., "Rewrite this marketing slogan for Chinese consumers")

*Critical Note:* While English output quality has improved significantly, complex technical or nuanced content may still be stronger in Chinese. Always verify critical translations with native speakers when accuracy is paramount.

## Use Cases

### 1. Academic Research & Literature Review
Tiangong AI excels at synthesizing information from multiple academic sources while maintaining proper citation practices. Researchers can input complex queries like "Compare recent studies on CRISPR-Cas9 ethical implications in European vs. US journals" and receive a structured summary with source links. The real-time data integration ensures access to the latest preprints and conference papers not yet in traditional databases. Students benefit from its ability to explain complex concepts in simpler terms and generate annotated bibliographies. For thesis writers, the platform's dual-response system helps verify claims against original sources, reducing the risk of academic misconduct.

### 2. Multilingual Content Marketing
Marketing teams targeting Chinese and global audiences find Tiangong AI invaluable for creating culturally resonant content. When developing campaigns for the Chinese market, it provides insights into local trends and platform-specific best practices (e.g., WeChat vs. Western social media). The translation feature goes beyond literal conversion, adapting idioms and cultural references appropriately. A notable use case involves generating product descriptions that comply with Chinese e-commerce regulations while maintaining brand voice – a task that typically requires specialized human translators. For global campaigns, users can create base content in English, then generate localized versions for 15+ languages with consistent messaging.

### 3. Developer Productivity & Learning
The code assistant serves as both a productivity tool and learning resource. Developers use it to:
- Generate boilerplate code for new projects with proper documentation
- Debug complex error messages by providing context-specific solutions
- Learn new frameworks through interactive explanations (e.g., "Explain React hooks with examples")
- Convert code between languages (e.g., "Convert this Python script to JavaScript")

Its real-time documentation integration means developers get accurate information about the latest library versions rather than relying on outdated model training data. Computer science students particularly benefit from the step-by-step code explanations, which break down complex algorithms into digestible concepts with visual examples. The platform also identifies security vulnerabilities in user-submitted code, providing fixes with explanations of why certain patterns are risky.

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- **Real-time information access**: Unlike many AI tools with static knowledge cutoffs, Tiangong AI provides current information through integrated search
- **Zero cost for core functionality**: Full access to search, content generation, and coding tools without payment
- **Multilingual proficiency**: Strong Chinese language processing with increasingly capable English output
- **Source transparency**: Every AI-generated response includes verifiable source links, building trust in results

**Cons:**
- **Language asymmetry**: English responses sometimes lack the nuance and depth of Chinese outputs, particularly for complex topics
- **Regional focus**: Some search results prioritize Chinese sources even for English queries, requiring additional filtering
- **No mobile application**: Only accessible via web browser, limiting on-the-go usage compared to competitors
- **Limited customization**: Fewer adjustable parameters than specialized tools like Jasper for content creation

The platform's strongest advantage is its search-integration approach, which solves a fundamental limitation of pure generative AI. However, users needing highly polished English content for professional publishing may still require human editing, especially for nuanced or creative work. The lack of mobile apps is a notable gap for users accustomed to AI assistants on their phones, though the responsive web design works reasonably well on mobile browsers.

## Alternatives

**1. Baidu ERNIE Bot**  
China's leading AI platform with strong language understanding but less robust search integration. Better for pure Chinese content creation but weaker for real-time information needs. Free tier available with similar query limits to Tiangong AI. Best for users focused exclusively on Chinese-language applications where search integration is less critical.

**2. Tongyi Qianwen (Qwen)**  
Alibaba's comprehensive AI model with strong coding capabilities and multilingual support. More developer-focused with API access in free tier. Lacks Tiangong's seamless search integration but offers better English output quality. Better for technical users needing to integrate AI into workflows via API.

**3. Bing AI (Microsoft Copilot)**  
Western alternative with strong English capabilities and Microsoft ecosystem integration. Offers image generation and more polished conversational abilities but has weaker Chinese language support. Requires Microsoft account; free but with stricter usage limits than Tiangong AI. Best for users already in the Microsoft ecosystem needing English-dominant assistance.

Each alternative has strengths in specific areas, but Tiangong AI remains unique in its search-first approach that prioritizes factual accuracy through real-time verification. For users needing both Chinese and English capabilities with current information, it currently offers the most balanced solution among Chinese AI platforms.
