## Overview

DALL-E prioritizes ease of use and out-of-box quality. Stable Diffusion emphasizes openness, customizability, and community-driven development.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | DALL-E | Stable Diffusion |
|---------|--------|------------------|
| Open Source | No | Yes |
| Local Deployment | No | Yes |
| Custom Models | No | Thousands (CivitAI) |
| Text Rendering | Excellent | Good |
| Ease of Use | Excellent | Moderate |
| Customizability | Limited | Extensive |
| Cost | ChatGPT Plus / API credit | Free (local GPU) |

## Verdict

Choose DALL-E for convenience and text-heavy images. Choose Stable Diffusion for maximum creative control, free generation, and custom model training.
