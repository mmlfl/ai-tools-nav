## Overview

The way we search for information is undergoing a profound shift. Traditional keyword-based search engines are being challenged by a new generation of AI-powered answer engines that deliver concise, sourced responses instead of just a list of links. Two notable contenders in this space are **Perplexity** and **Tiangong AI** (天工 AI). While both fall under the broad “AI search” category, they represent very different philosophies, target audiences, and feature sets.

Perplexity has rapidly established itself as the go‑to research assistant for users who demand accuracy, transparency, and real‑time web grounding. Built around a conversational search interface, it provides answers with inline citations, making fact‑checking effortless. In 2026, Perplexity has expanded far beyond simple Q&A, adding Deep Research, file analysis, and even AI image generation — all while keeping its core identity as a citation‑first search engine. Its freemium model offers a generous free tier, but power users gravitate toward the $20/month Pro plan for unlimited advanced searches.

Tiangong AI, developed by Chinese tech company Kunlun Tech, takes a fundamentally different approach. It is not merely a search engine but an all‑in‑one creation platform that integrates search, writing, coding, image generation, and document processing into a single, free package. Designed primarily for the Chinese‑speaking market, Tiangong AI aims to be a daily companion for students, professionals, and creators who want a Swiss Army knife of AI tools without paying a subscription. The platform is built on Kunlun’s proprietary large language models and emphasizes multimodal capabilities, making it a versatile hub rather than a pure research tool.

In this comparison, we’ll examine how these two tools stack up in features, pricing, and ideal use cases, helping you decide which one deserves a place in your digital toolkit.

## Feature Comparison

At first glance, both Perplexity and Tiangong AI offer AI‑powered search, but the depth and breadth of their features differ significantly. Perplexity is laser‑focused on delivering reliable, well‑sourced answers, while Tiangong AI tries to replace multiple standalone tools with a unified platform.

| Feature | Perplexity | Tiangong AI |
|---------|------------|-------------|
| **Core Search Experience** | Conversational search with real‑time web grounding. Every answer includes numbered citations linked to original sources. Pro users can choose from multiple AI models (GPT‑4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, etc.) for the search backend. | AI‑enhanced search that combines traditional web results with generated summaries. Citations are present but less prominent; the interface leans toward a chatbot style that can also create images, write code, or draft documents directly from the search bar. |
| **AI Models & Customisation** | Free tier uses Perplexity’s default model optimised for speed. Pro subscribers unlock access to leading third‑party models (OpenAI, Anthropic) and can switch between them for different tasks. A “Writing” mode tones down search behaviour for creative tasks. | Powered by Kunlun’s in‑house Skywork large models. The assistant automatically determines the best internal model for the task (search, writing, coding) without manual switching. No third‑party model integration, but the platform is deeply optimised for Chinese language and multimodal output. |
| **Multimodal & Creation Tools** | Primarily text‑centric. Pro users can upload images, PDFs, and code files for analysis. A built‑in “Playground” offers AI image generation (powered by models like DALL‑E 3 and Stable Diffusion), but it is a separate tab from the main search interface. | Fully multimodal from the ground up. Users can generate images, write and debug code, compose essays, create presentations, and summarise documents — all within the same chat thread. Beyond the table, it’s worth noting that Perplexity’s “Deep Research” feature (available to Pro users) can autonomously browse dozens of web pages, synthesise information, and produce a detailed, cited report — a capability that mimics a junior research analyst. Tiangong AI does not offer an equivalent autonomous research agent, but it compensates with a broader set of creative tools that can generate everything from social media copy to functional Python scripts without leaving the chat.

## Pricing Comparison

Pricing is perhaps the most dramatic difference between these two platforms. Perplexity operates on a freemium model with a clear upgrade path, while Tiangong AI is currently entirely free, betting on user growth and ecosystem lock‑in.

| Plan | Perplexity | Tiangong AI |
|------|------------|-------------|
| **Free Tier** | Unlimited quick searches; limited “Pro” searches (5 per day as of 2026). Access to the default model only. File upload not included. | All features are free: unlimited searches, writing, coding, image generation, and file analysis. No daily caps on usage. Ad‑supported or subsidised by Kunlun Tech’s broader business. |
| **Premium / Pro** | **Perplexity Pro**: $20/month or $200/year (annual billing saves ~17%). Includes unlimited Pro searches, choice of AI models (GPT‑4o, Claude, etc.), file upload & analysis, Deep Research, and image generation playground. | Currently no premium tier. The entire platform is free, with no indication of an upcoming paid plan. Kunlun Tech may monetise through enterprise services or B2B APIs in the future. |
| **Team / Enterprise** | Enterprise plans with centralised billing, admin controls, and higher usage limits are available for organisations. Pricing is custom. | Not available for teams. The tool is positioned for individual consumers and does not offer enterprise‑grade administration. |
| **Free Trial of Pro** | New users can try Perplexity Pro free for 7 days. Occasionally, promotions extend this to 1 month. | Not applicable — the entire service is free from the moment you sign up. No credit card required. |

*Table 2: Pricing comparison between Perplexity and Tiangong AI.*

The zero‑cost nature of Tiangong AI is undeniably attractive, especially for students or casual users who need an occasional AI assistant. However, the old adage “you get what you pay for” sometimes applies. Perplexity’s subscription funds continuous model upgrades, a robust citation infrastructure, and access to expensive third‑party models that can significantly improve answer quality for complex queries. Tiangong AI’s free model relies on Kunlun Tech’s own models, which, while competent, may not match the cutting‑edge performance of GPT‑4o or Claude 3.5 Sonnet on nuanced English‑language tasks.

## Use Cases

Choosing between Perplexity and Tiangong AI ultimately depends on what you need to accomplish and the linguistic environment you work in.

### When to Choose Perplexity

- **Academic Research & Fact‑Checking**: If you’re writing a paper, preparing a report, or verifying claims, Perplexity’s rigorous citation system is invaluable. Every statement can be traced back to its original source, making it easy to evaluate credibility and avoid plagiarism.
- **Professional Knowledge Work**: Consultants, journalists, and analysts who need to quickly synthesise information from multiple sources will appreciate Perplexity’s Deep Research and the ability to switch between AI models for different perspectives.
- **English‑Language Dominance**: For users who primarily search English‑language websites, academic journals, and global news outlets, Perplexity’s indexing and model optimisation deliver noticeably better results.
- **Privacy‑Conscious Users**: Perplexity offers a clear privacy policy and does not require a phone number for sign‑up. The paid plan ensures you are the customer, not the product.

### When to Choose Tiangong AI

- **Chinese‑Language Ecosystem**: If your work or studies revolve around Chinese‑language content — Baidu, Zhihu, WeChat Official Accounts, Chinese government documents — Tiangong AI’s search and generation quality is superior. It understands the nuances of Simplified Chinese and the local web landscape far better than Perplexity.
- **All‑in‑One Daily Assistant**: Tiangong AI replaces multiple tools. Need to search for a recipe, then write a shopping list, then generate an image for a party invitation? You can do it all in one chat thread without switching apps.
- **Budget‑Conscious Users**: For those who cannot or do not want to pay a monthly subscription, Tiangong AI offers a remarkably complete feature set for free. It’s hard to beat the value proposition of unlimited image generation, coding help, and document analysis at zero cost.
- **Creative Brainstorming in Chinese**: The platform’s writing and image generation capabilities are deeply integrated, making it a fun sandbox for content creators, marketers, and students working in Chinese.

## Verdict & Recommendation

Perplexity and Tiangong AI are not direct competitors in the traditional sense — they are more like two tools that happen to overlap on the Venn diagram of AI search. Perplexity is the disciplined researcher: meticulous, source‑obsessed, and willing to pay for the best available models to ensure accuracy. Tiangong AI is the versatile creator: free‑spirited, multimodal, and deeply embedded in the Chinese digital ecosystem.

For English‑speaking professionals, researchers, and anyone whose work depends on verifiable facts, **Perplexity is the clear winner**. Its subscription fee is easily justified by the time saved fact‑checking and the confidence that comes from seeing exactly where information originated. The ability to tap into multiple frontier AI models also future‑proofs the tool as the AI landscape evolves.

For Chinese‑speaking users who want a free, jack‑of‑all‑trades assistant that can search, write, code, and create images in one place, **Tiangong AI is an excellent choice**. It eliminates the need to juggle multiple freemium tools, and its understanding of the Chinese internet is unmatched by global alternatives. Students, casual users, and creators on a budget will find it an indispensable daily companion.

If your needs straddle both worlds — perhaps you are bilingual and require deep research in English but also create content in Chinese — using both tools in tandem is a viable strategy. Perplexity can handle the heavy‑lifting research, while Tiangong AI can assist with Chinese‑language writing and creative tasks.

Ultimately, the “best” tool is the one that aligns with your language, workflow, and budget. In 2026, both Perplexity and Tiangong AI demonstrate that AI search is no longer a novelty — it’s a fundamental utility, and the competition is only making it better for users everywhere.
