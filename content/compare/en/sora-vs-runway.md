## Overview

Sora (OpenAI) made headlines with remarkably realistic video generation from text. Runway offers a comprehensive creative suite with video generation, editing, and VFX tools.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | Sora | Runway |
|---------|------|--------|
| Video Quality | Excellent | Excellent |
| Max Video Length | Up to 60 seconds | Up to 18 seconds (Gen-3) |
| Video Editing | Basic | Advanced (full suite) |
| Motion Brush | No | Yes |
| Green Screen | No | Yes |
| API Access | Limited | Yes |
| Availability | ChatGPT Plus/Pro | Web + API |

## Verdict

Sora leads in pure generation quality and length for complex realistic scenes. Runway offers a more complete creative toolset for professional video production workflows.
