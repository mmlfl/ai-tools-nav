## Overview

The landscape of AI-powered search engines in China is evolving rapidly, with two standout platforms capturing the attention of researchers, students, and creators alike: **MetaSo AI (秘塔 AI)** and **Tiangong AI (天工 AI)**. Both tools promise to redefine how we find and synthesize information, yet they approach the challenge from distinctly different angles.

MetaSo AI positions itself as a pure, ad-free answer engine built for deep research and knowledge organization. Its hallmark is delivering structured, citation-backed answers and automatically generated mind maps, making it a favorite among academics, analysts, and anyone who needs to dive deep into a topic without distraction. By focusing exclusively on search and research, MetaSo has earned praise for its clarity, depth, and lack of commercial clutter.

Tiangong AI, developed by Kunlun Tech, takes a much broader approach. It is not just a search engine but a multi-functional large-model assistant that integrates AI search, writing, coding, and creative tools into a single platform. This all-in-one design aims to be a daily companion for a wide range of tasks—from drafting essays and debugging code to generating images and answering complex questions. With its expansive feature set, Tiangong targets users who want a Swiss Army knife rather than a scalpel.

In this comparison, we’ll dissect the strengths and weaknesses of both platforms across features, pricing, and ideal use cases, helping you decide which AI companion best fits your workflow in 2026.

## Feature Comparison

While both tools fall under the “AI search” umbrella, their feature sets diverge dramatically. The table below outlines the core capabilities side by side.

| Feature                  | MetaSo AI                                       | Tiangong AI                                      |
|--------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| **Core Function**        | AI answer engine & deep research                | AI search, writing, coding & creation platform   |
| **Search Capabilities**  | Web search with structured answers              | Web search with conversational AI                |
| **Answer Format**        | Structured summaries, key points, and mind maps | Conversational text, documents, code blocks      |
| **Mind Map Generation**  | Automatic, from any search query                | Not available                                    |
| **Writing Assistant**    | Limited to research summaries                   | Full writing tools: essays, reports, emails, etc.|
| **Coding Support**       | Not supported                                   | Code generation, debugging, explanation          |
| **Multimodal Input**     | Text only                                       | Text, image upload, and voice input              |
| **Source Citations**     | Yes, with clickable references                  | Yes, often with source links                     |
| **Ad-Free Experience**   | Completely ad-free                              | Free, may include promotional content            |
| **Language Support**     | Primarily Chinese, some English                 | Chinese and English                              |
| **User Interface**       | Clean, minimal, research-focused                | Feature-rich dashboard with multiple modules     |

**Digging deeper:**

- **MetaSo AI** excels at transforming a simple query into a well-organized research brief. Its mind maps are not just visual gimmicks; they help users see connections between concepts, which is invaluable for learning and brainstorming. The ad-free environment ensures zero distractions, and every claim is backed by a source you can verify instantly. However, if your task goes beyond research—say, writing a full article or coding a script—MetaSo leaves you to find other tools.
- **Tiangong AI** counters with versatility. You can start with a search, ask the AI to expand it into a blog post, switch to a coding problem, and then generate an illustration—all within the same interface. This integration saves time and context switching. The trade-off is that none of its individual features are as deep as a dedicated tool; the search answers may not be as meticulously structured as MetaSo’s, and the interface can feel busy if you only need quick facts.

## Use Cases

Choosing between MetaSo AI and Tiangong AI comes down to what you intend to accomplish. Below are the scenarios where each tool shines.

### When to choose MetaSo AI

- **Academic & Deep Research:** You need to explore a topic thoroughly, gather citations, and understand the conceptual landscape. The automatic mind map helps you visualize the structure of knowledge, and ad-free search ensures you’re not swayed by sponsored content.
- **Fact-Checking & Analysis:** Journalists, analysts, and consultants who require quick, verifiable summaries with direct source links will find MetaSo’s structured answers a massive time-saver.
- **Learning & Knowledge Management:** Students can input a complex question and receive a digestible breakdown plus a mind map that they can save or share. It’s like having a research assistant that organizes your thoughts.

**Pros of MetaSo AI:**  
- Unmatched depth in research-oriented queries  
- Distraction-free, ad-free interface  
- Unique mind map generation that no competitor offers  
- Strong source transparency  

**Cons of MetaSo AI:**  
- No writing, coding, or creative tools  
- Limited to text input; cannot handle images or voice  
- Primarily Chinese-language optimization (English support is secondary)  

### When to choose Tiangong AI

- **Content Creation & Copywriting:** Bloggers, marketers, and social media managers can generate articles, ad copy, or video scripts directly from search results, then refine them with built-in writing tools.
- **Software Development & Learning:** Developers can ask coding questions, get code snippets, and debug errors without leaving the platform. The conversational AI understands context, making it a handy coding companion.
- **Everyday AI Assistant:** For users who want one tool to answer questions, draft emails, summarize documents, and even create images, Tiangong’s all-in-one design reduces the need to juggle multiple apps.

**Pros of Tiangong AI:**  
- Incredible versatility across search, writing, and coding  
- Multimodal input (text, image, voice) broadens usability  
- Integrated workflow reduces context switching  
- Strong Chinese and English language support  

**Cons of Tiangong AI:**  
- Search results may lack the deep structure and mind maps of MetaSo  
- Interface can feel cluttered with so many features  
- Long-term sustainability of free access is uncertain  
- Less specialized for pure research tasks  

## Verdict & Recommendation

After a thorough comparison, the choice between MetaSo AI and Tiangong AI is not about which tool is better overall, but which aligns with your primary needs.

If your daily work revolves around **in-depth research, knowledge synthesis, and verifying information**, MetaSo AI is the clear winner. Its structured answers, source citations, and mind maps offer a level of clarity and focus that no general-purpose platform can match. The fact that it remains completely free and ad-free makes it an indispensable tool for students, academics, and professionals who value truth over convenience.

On the other hand, if you are a **creator, developer, or someone who wants a single AI companion for a wide range of tasks**, Tiangong AI is the more practical choice. Its ability to seamlessly switch from search to writing to coding—and even accept images and voice—makes it a powerful productivity hub. The trade-off in research depth is a fair price for the versatility it brings.

For many users, the ideal workflow might involve **using both tools in tandem**: MetaSo for the initial deep dive and knowledge mapping, then Tiangong to transform that research into polished content, code, or creative output. Given that both are free, there’s no reason not to experiment and find the combination that suits you best.

In 2026, the AI landscape continues to reward specialization as much as integration. MetaSo AI and Tiangong AI exemplify these two philosophies, and your choice will ultimately reflect whether you value depth or breadth in your AI-powered toolkit.
