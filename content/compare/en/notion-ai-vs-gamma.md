## Overview

Notion AI enhances the Notion workspace with AI writing and Q&A capabilities. Gamma is a dedicated AI presentation and document builder focused on visual appeal.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | Notion AI | Gamma |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Primary Use | Writing, notes, databases | Presentations, docs, web pages |
| Platform | Notion workspace | Standalone web app |
| Presentations | No (doc-based) | Yes (core feature) |
| Visual Design | Templates | AI-generated layouts |
| Database/Analytics | Yes (Notion core) | No |
| Pricing | $10/month add-on | Free / $10 Pro |

## Verdict

Notion AI is the natural choice for Notion users who want AI in their existing workspace. Gamma is better for creating visually impressive presentations and documents from scratch.
