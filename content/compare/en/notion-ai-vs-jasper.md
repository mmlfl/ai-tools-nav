## Overview

Notion AI is an AI layer within the Notion workspace. Jasper is a dedicated AI marketing platform for content teams creating at scale.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | Notion AI | Jasper |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Focus | Workspace AI assistant | Marketing content platform |
| Brand Voice | Basic | Advanced (Brand Voice) |
| SEO Tools | No | Yes (Jasper SEO) |
| Campaign Management | No | Yes |
| API | No | Yes |
| Pricing | $10/month (add-on) | $49/month (Creator) |

## Verdict

Notion AI for individuals and teams already using Notion. Jasper for professional marketing teams needing specialized content tools with brand controls and SEO features.
