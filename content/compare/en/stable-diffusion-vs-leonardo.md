## Overview

Stable Diffusion is the open-source foundation with unlimited flexibility. Leonardo is a managed platform that adds fine-tuning, community models, and a polished web interface.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | Stable Diffusion | Leonardo |
|---------|------------------|----------|
| Deployment | Local (free) or cloud | Cloud (managed) |
| Open Source | Yes | Built on SD |
| Custom Models | Thousands (CivitAI) | Community + custom training |
| Ease of Use | Moderate | Easy |
| ControlNet | Yes (extensive) | Limited |
| Cost | Free (local GPU) | Free tier / Paid plans |

## Verdict

Stable Diffusion for power users who want maximum control and have technical skills. Leonardo for users who want Stable Diffusion's power through a polished, easy-to-use platform with built-in fine-tuning.
