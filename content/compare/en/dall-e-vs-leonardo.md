## Overview

DALL-E (OpenAI) and Leonardo AI are two popular AI image tools. DALL-E is known for ChatGPT integration and strong text rendering. Leonardo specializes in artistic generation with fine-grained control and custom model training.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | DALL-E | Leonardo |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Core Model | DALL-E 3 | Phoenix / Lightning |
| Image Quality | Excellent | Excellent |
| Text Rendering | Best-in-class | Good |
| Artistic Styles | Good | Excellent |
| Fine-tuning | No | Yes |
| ChatGPT Integration | Native | No |
| API | Yes | Yes |

## Verdict

Choose DALL-E for general image generation, text-heavy images, and ChatGPT integration. Choose Leonardo for artistic projects, game assets, and custom model training.
