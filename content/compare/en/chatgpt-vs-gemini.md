## Overview

ChatGPT (OpenAI) and Gemini (Google) are two of the most powerful AI assistants in 2026. ChatGPT leads in ecosystem breadth and creative capabilities, while Gemini leverages Google's massive search index and Workspace integration.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | ChatGPT | Gemini |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Core Model | GPT-4o / GPT-4o mini | Gemini 2.5 Pro / Flash |
| Context Window | 128K tokens | Up to 1M tokens |
| Text Generation | ★★★★★ | ★★★★★ |
| Coding | ★★★★★ | ★★★★ |
| Multimodal Input | Image, audio | Image, audio, video |
| Web Search | Yes (GPT-4o) | Yes (native) |
| Image Generation | DALL·E 3 | Imagen 3 |
| Google Integration | No | Gmail, Docs, Sheets |

## Pricing

| Plan | ChatGPT | Gemini |
|------|---------|--------|
| Free | GPT-4o mini | Gemini 2.5 Flash |
| Premium | $20/month (Plus) | $19.99/month (Google One) |
| Business | $25/user/month | Varies |

## Verdict

Choose ChatGPT for creative work, coding, and the GPT Store ecosystem. Choose Gemini if you're deep in Google Workspace and want seamless integration with Gmail, Docs, and Sheets.
