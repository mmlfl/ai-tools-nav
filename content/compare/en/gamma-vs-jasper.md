## Overview

Gamma specializes in AI presentations, documents, and web pages. Jasper focuses on marketing copy, blog posts, and brand-aligned content creation at scale.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | Gamma | Jasper |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Primary Output | Presentations, docs, web pages | Marketing copy, blogs, ads |
| Brand Voice | Basic | Advanced (Brand Voice) |
| SEO Tools | No | Yes (Jasper SEO) |
| Templates | Extensive visual templates | 50+ marketing templates |
| Collaboration | Real-time editing | Team workflows |
| Pricing | Free / $10 Pro | $49 Creator |

## Verdict

Gamma excels at visual content creation - presentations, docs, and simple websites. Jasper dominates marketing content workflows with brand voice and SEO tools. Choose based on whether you need visual documents or marketing copy.
