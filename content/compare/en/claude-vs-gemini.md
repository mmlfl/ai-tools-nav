## Overview

Claude (Anthropic) and Gemini (Google) represent two distinct philosophies in AI development. Claude prioritizes safety, deep reasoning, and long-context understanding. Gemini emphasizes multimodal capabilities, Google ecosystem integration, and massive scale.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | Claude | Gemini |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Core Model | Claude 3.5 Sonnet / Opus | Gemini 2.5 Pro / Flash |
| Context Window | 200K tokens | Up to 1M tokens |
| Reasoning Depth | ★★★★★ | ★★★★ |
| Coding | ★★★★★ | ★★★★ |
| Multimodal | Image | Image, audio, video |
| Web Search | No | Yes (native) |
| Google Integration | No | Full Workspace |
| Safety | Constitutional AI | Google AI Principles |

## Verdict

Claude wins on pure reasoning quality and long-document analysis. Gemini wins on ecosystem breadth and multimodal capabilities. For developers and researchers, Claude is often preferred. For Google Workspace users, Gemini is the natural choice.
