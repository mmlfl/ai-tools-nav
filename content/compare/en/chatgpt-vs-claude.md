## Overview

ChatGPT and Claude are the two leading AI chat assistants in 2026, developed by OpenAI and Anthropic respectively. Both are rapidly evolving and each has distinct strengths.

ChatGPT excels with its extensive ecosystem — web search, DALL·E image generation, Code Interpreter, and GPT Store. Claude stands out with its 200K token context window and deep reasoning capabilities, ideal for complex long-form document analysis.

Choose ChatGPT for general productivity with broad capabilities, web search, and DALL·E integration. Choose Claude for deep analytical work, long-document processing, and safety-critical applications. Many professionals subscribe to both.
