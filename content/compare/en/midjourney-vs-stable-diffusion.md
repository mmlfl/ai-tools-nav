## Overview

Midjourney offers the best out-of-box quality with a curated experience. Stable Diffusion provides complete freedom, customizability, and free local deployment.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | Midjourney | Stable Diffusion |
|---------|------------|------------------|
| Open Source | No | Yes |
| Local Deployment | No | Yes |
| Out-of-box Quality | Excellent | Good |
| Custom Models | No | Thousands (CivitAI) |
| ControlNet | Limited | Extensive |
| Cost | $10-60/month | Free (local GPU) |
| Privacy | Cloud-based | Full local privacy |

## Verdict

Midjourney for convenience and consistently beautiful results without tinkering. Stable Diffusion for maximum creative control, free unlimited generation, and complete workflow flexibility. Many serious creators use both.
